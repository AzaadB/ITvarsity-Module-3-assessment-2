Paneer Tikka

In Indian cuisine, Paneer Tikka — also known as Tandoori Tikka or grilled paneer — is grilled with spices and yogurt (or dahi) marinated in cubes of cottage cheese. After marinating the paneer cubes and vegetables, they are arranged on skewers and grilled. This dish's flavor comes from the marinade made with aromatic spices.



Price: R150


Serving Size: 6
