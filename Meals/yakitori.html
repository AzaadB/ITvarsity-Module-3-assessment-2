Yakitori

Yakitori (pronounced'-kitori') refers to grilled chicken skewers made from bite-size cuts of meat from chicken parts such as breasts, thighs, skin, liver, and other innards. A popular, inexpensive dish enjoyed with a glass of beer, yakitori is typically made to order and cooked over charcoal.



Price: R25,73


Serving Size: 6
